DUMMY LEG LENGTH CALCULATOR HELP

Calculates the long point and throat dimensions for pipe dummy legs attached to pipe or elbow

Enter header and dummy leg pipe/fitting NPS in decimal inches ( 1.25, 2, 3.5, 12, etc )

For "L" dimension, any unneeded fields may be left blank ( you don't have to enter a "0" )

NOTE: All calculations rounded to the nearest 1/16th of an inch
